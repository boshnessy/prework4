Spiked Caramel Apple Cider Recipe

Ingredients

Cider

  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 cups apple cider
  • 1 shot Caramel Vodka, per mug
  • 1 shot Butterscotch Schnapps, per mug
Do not add alcohol to saucepan while cooking!

Garnish, if desired

  • Additional whipping cream, whipped
  • Caramel topping
  • Ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In 2-quart saucepan, stir together brown sugar and 1/3 cup whipping cream.
  2. Cook over medium heat 2 minutes, stirring constantly, until bubbly.
  3. Stir in vanilla and apple cider.
  4. Cook 10 minutes, stirring often, until thoroughly heated.
  5. Serve cider in mugs.
  6. Add Vodka and Schnapps.
  7. Top with whipped cream, caramel topping and/or cinnamon.
Enjoy!
